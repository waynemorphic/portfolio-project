About Wayne

I am Wayne Kirimi, a technology enthusiast with an eye for fluid and modern designs. I studied for a social science degree for my undergraduate degree but decided to switch careers into IT since I belive this is where my passion lies. I believe that, when taking a lifelong career decision, considering the things you are passionate about is key. I have no prior pragramming job experience and I hope and believe that, if I work hard in this course at Moringa school, I will become an adept software developer. Therefore, I decided to join Moringa school since the school has a clear roadmap of what they expect their students to achieve as software developers and their objective of getting more Africans into the technology ecosytem resonates well with me. Taking a programming class in a hands on school like Moringa school will equip me with profecient tech skills that I will harness in commercial and open source software development after graduatng successfully.
